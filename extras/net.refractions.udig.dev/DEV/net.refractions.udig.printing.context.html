net.refractions.udig.printing.context

The Printing Context is mostly the same as a normal net.refractions.udig.project.context. The main difference lies in its intended use.

  • Selection information has been broken into separate (specific) layers.

A net.refractions.udig.printing.page may contain several Printing Contexts based on its original net.refractions.udig.printing.template.
