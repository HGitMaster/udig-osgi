The error log in uDig can be submitted to the uDig development team in 3 easy clicks by selecting Help > Submit Log... > Proceed. Please include some notes describing the nature of the problem and the steps required to reproduce it. Optionally, you may include contact information.

The uDig bug tracker (JIRA) is available at: http://jira.codehaus.org/browse/UDIG

If the issue you have uncovered is not present here, please consider adding it. You may also "watch" an issue to learn when it has been resolved.
